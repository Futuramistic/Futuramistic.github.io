To make high-quality 3D Gaussian Splatting (GS) viable for computationally constrained devices, we introduce <strong>Trick-GS</strong>, which integrates progressive training, significance-based pruning, and an accelerated training framework. This approach achieves comparable accuracy to vanilla GS while delivering up to 2x faster training and rendering speeds, alongside a 40x reduction in model size.
